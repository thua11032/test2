ABOUT ME

Hello! I am currently a senior mechanical engineering student at Stanford University graduating in June 2018.

From a very young age, my greasemonkey personality complemented by my hobbyist's room decided that engineering was going to be my career. I absolutely love the process of ideating, designing, and prototyping solutions. I am very excited for all the new possibilities that technology can bring for us and I look forward to making my own contributions! Smart consumer electronics, autonomous vehicles, sustainable technologies, robotics... I can't wait to help create the future!

I am always on the lookout for the next project, whether it be a weekend hack or a year long kickstarter. If I look like a good fit for your project, I'd love to hear from you!.
